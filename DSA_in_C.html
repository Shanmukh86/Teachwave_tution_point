Introduction to DSA

This topic introduces the fundamentals of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and their importance in programming and problem-solving.

Arrays

Learn about arrays, how to declare and manipulate them in C, and explore common array operations like traversal, insertion, and deletion.

Linked Lists

Understand linked lists, their types (singly, doubly), and their applications. Learn operations like insertion, deletion, and traversal.

Stacks

Study stack data structure and its operations such as push, pop, and peek. Understand how stacks are used in algorithmic problem solving.

Queues

Learn about queues, including different types like circular and priority queues. Explore operations like enqueue, dequeue, and front.

Trees

Get an introduction to tree data structures, including binary trees, binary search trees, and tree traversal techniques (in-order, pre-order, post-order).

Graphs

Study graph data structure, types of graphs (directed, undirected), and algorithms for graph traversal such as BFS and DFS.

Sorting Algorithms

Explore popular sorting algorithms like Bubble Sort, Selection Sort, Insertion Sort, Merge Sort, and Quick Sort, and understand their complexities.

Searching Algorithms

Learn about searching algorithms such as Linear Search and Binary Search, and their applications in efficient data retrieval.
